About me

Builder by instinct,
engineer by craft.

I'm a software engineer and AI builder with a deep background spanning machine learning research, founding-stage startups, and production-scale systems. My work lives at the intersection of AI, cloud infrastructure, and user-centric product design.

Most recently I'm driving a global healthcare interoperability platform at Kai Nest, building around openEHR/FHIR standards across five countries. Before that, I was a Founding Engineer at Mem0 (formerly Embedchain), where I helped build an open-source RAG framework that's gathered over 40,000 stars on GitHub.

I hold a Master of Engineering in Computer Science from Penn State, where I also did research at the Deep Learning Lab on landslide prediction and at the Undergraduate Deep Learning Lab on telescope image analysis.

Outside of work I recharge on the football pitch, cycling trails, and in books. I believe movement and curiosity are the best complements to a life in tech.
